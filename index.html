Quite possibly the most awesome individual to ever grace this planet known as Earth-- TJ Estrella is a man who knows of no bounds! Born July 22nd, 1986 in Manhattan, NY, Terenzio Joseph (with many other names before his last) Estrella came out the womb kicking ass and taking names. Growing up as a very awesome kid, T.J. would go on to do many great things in life. A US Marine from the East Coast, this Devil Dog currently works as a site surveyor and designer for a solar and roofing company.
